OUR STORY
Juana’s Tea began in 2023 as a small family venture in Brgy. Gulang-Gulang, crafting homemade drinks from their own home. Thanks to passion, hard work, and loyal customers, the business quickly grew—leading to the opening of their first stall at Gulang-Gulang National High School (GGNHS) in 2025.
The Juana Experience
At Juana, every sip and every bite tells a story of comfort, creativity, and Filipino warmth. What started as a small neighborhood spot quickly became a beloved hangout where friends gather, students unwind, and families enjoy their favorites. From our creamy milk teas to refreshing fruit teas, from crispy fries to satisfying burgers, and our signature graham bars, everything we serve is made with care and crafted to bring joy in every order. Juana isn’t just a store — it’s a place where flavors meet memories.
Where Flavor Meets Comfort
At Juana, we believe good food should welcome all tastes. Whether you’re craving something sweet, fruity, savory, or indulgent, our menu has something for every mood. From rich and creamy milk teas to vibrant fruit teas, golden crispy fries, hearty burgers, and our signature graham bars, each item is crafted with comfort and creativity in mind. Nestled at the heart of the community, Juana is your go-to spot for everyday cravings and special treats alike.
MISSION & VISION
Mission
Our mission is to be the daily comfort and little joy in our neighborhood. We strive to craft every cup with the highest quality, freshest ingredients because our customers deserve the best.
Vision
Our vision is to become a well-loved local brand known for quality, passion, and heart. We dream of expanding ‘Juana’ to serve more people while staying true to our roots — a family business built on love, dedication, and genuine care for every customer.
