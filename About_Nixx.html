Juana's Tea began in 2023 as a small family business in Unzon Ibaba TMPI-1, Brgy. Gulang-Gulang. The family started by selling homemade products from their own home. With hard work, passion, and loyal customers, Juana's Tea grew and by 2025 opened its first stall at Gulang-Gulang National High School (GGNHS).
